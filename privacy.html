Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2025

MailMind is a Chrome Extension that helps users improve their email productivity by summarizing Gmail threads and generating AI-assisted reply suggestions. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to handling your data responsibly.

1. Data We Access

MailMind accesses the content of your Gmail messages only while you are using the extension. This access is required to display email threads and generate summaries or replies using AI.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect or Store

  • We do not store or permanently collect any of your emails or personal data.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Use of External APIs

MailMind may temporarily send email content to an AI API (e.g., OpenAI) only for the purpose of generating summaries and replies. These requests are not stored or retained by MailMind.

4. Permissions

MailMind requests access only to mail.google.com and the necessary data to perform its functions. We do not request unnecessary permissions.

5. Your Consent

By using MailMind, you consent to this privacy policy.
